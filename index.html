Welcome to my website which will take you on a journey through the improvements and advancements made in gaming. From simple 8-bit games to advanced VR gaming.

The genres page will cover some simple gaming genres and how most games now are not tied down to a single genre but instead pull inspiration from many different genres.

The how graphics have improved over time page will compare old 8-bit games against modern games and talk about how the graphics have improved and changed over time.

Visual styles will cover some basic and unique styles which modern games use from photorealism to Cel-shading.

VR gaming has some videos showing off the current state of VR gaming which I have made myself and will also talk about how VR gaming has made massive strides in development over such a short time.
